This is the best and fastest way to reach out to me and get class community support. Use this Signup link and type your @spu.edu email: sputwp.slack.com/signup Use of mobile/desktop apps is strongly recommended. Search in app/play store or get more information at slack.com/downloads.

Once you sign-up, you can use sputwp.slack.com to open the slack channel.

Get to the class channel #csc2230-winter19 and ask questions there. It is a shared classroom messenger channel for all students in my classes.
